Since this product is a bit expensive and you for sure want to have some kind of protection, here is idea you could use for your device. </p>\n\n<p>I have heard that some people had luck to have someone who can build special purpose case(s) from aluminum, or have patience to wait for special China delivery (for months), but for those who are in no luck, we can simply use some 5mm <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Poly%28methyl_methacrylate%29" rel="nofollow">Acrylic Glass</a> as it\xe2\x80\x99s very common, cheap, easy to cut, engrave and provides fair protection for packing and traveling. </p>\n\n<p>First, get one of these vector files I have created for cutting and if you like engraving of Acrylic Glass sheets. Here are 3 vector files (same), a <a href="http://www.gerzic.com/media/B200/b200.eps" rel="nofollow">EPS</a>, <a href="http://www.gerzic.com/media/B200/b200.svg" rel="nofollow">SVG</a> and <a href="http://www.gerzic.com/media/B200/b200.pdf" rel="nofollow">PDF</a> versions just in case. Using Acrylic Glass was not my idea, first time I have seen it, was from <a href="http://www.voja.rs" rel="nofollow">Voja Antonic</a> who build similar case for his <a href="http://www.voja.rs/PROJECTS/GAME_HTM/1_intro.htm" rel="nofollow">retro gaming console</a>.</p>\n\n<p>It will look something like this:<br>\n<a href="https://svbtleusercontent.com/ao6i7daulrl1w.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://svbtleusercontent.com/ao6i7daulrl1w_small.jpg" alt="b200_01.jpg"></a></p>\n\n<p>My choice for screws was that bottom ones will be 10mm and top ones 20mm as that\xe2\x80\x99s enough space for B200 to be naturally cooled. After assembly it will look (hopefully) like this: </p>\n\n<p><a href="https://svbtleusercontent.com/yith78gpke3xvw.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://svbtleusercontent.com/yith78gpke3xvw_small.jpg" alt="b200_05.jpg"></a></p>\n\n<p>Since it\xe2\x80\x99s 45mm \xe2\x80\x9cfat\xe2\x80\x9d it can stand by side like this:<br>\n<a href="https://svbtleusercontent.com/bhqethcjrxxw.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://svbtleusercontent.com/bhqethcjrxxw_small.jpg" alt="b200_03.jpg"></a></p>\n\n<p>Bottom pic:<br>\n<a href="https://svbtleusercontent.com/t0hdyid7t1nl7w.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://svbtleusercontent.com/t0hdyid7t1nl7w_small.jpg" alt="b200_06.jpg"></a></p>\n\n<p>I do hope this helps you out and if so I would like to see your pictures of devices after they are done. href="//blog.gerzic.rs/ettus-research-b200-simple-case">Ettus Research USRP B200/B210 simple case</a>\n  </h1>\n\t<p>Hi there. If you are into <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Software-defined_radio" rel="nofollow">SDR</a> and out looking for signals, you have probably heard about <a href="http://www.ettus.com/product" rel="nofollow">Ettus Research products</a>. </p>\n\n<p>In case you have bought B200 or B210 product you know that these will be delivered without any case/box. Since this product is a bit expensive and you for sure want to have some kind of protection, here is idea you could use for your device. </p>\n\n<p>I have heard that some people had luck to have someone who can build special purpose case(s) from aluminum, or have patience to wait for special China delivery (for months), but for those who are in no luck, we can simply use some 5mm <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Poly%28methyl_methacrylate%29" rel="nofollow">Acrylic Glass</a> as it\xe2\x80\x99s very common, cheap, easy to cut, engrave and provides fair protection for packing and traveling. </p>\n\n<p>First, get one of these vector files I have created for cutting and if you like engraving of Acrylic Glass sheets. You can give these to anyone who has Laser engraving services. Here are 3 vector files (same), a <a href="http://www.gerzic.com/media/B200/b200.eps" rel="nofollow">EPS</a>, <a href="http://www.gerzic.com/media/B200/b200.svg" rel="nofollow">SVG</a> and <a href="http://www.gerzic.com/media/B200/b200.pdf" rel="nofollow">PDF</a> versions just in case. Using Acrylic Glass was not my idea, first time I have seen it, was from <a href="http://www.voja.rs" rel="nofollow">Voja Antonic</a> who build similar case for his <a href="http://www.voja.rs/PROJECTS/GAME_HTM/1_intro.htm" rel="nofollow">retro gaming console</a>.</p>\n\n<p>It will look something like this:<br>\n<a href="https://svbtleusercontent.com/ao6i7daulrl1w.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://svbtleusercontent.com/ao6i7daulrl1w_small.jpg" alt="b200_01.jpg"></a></p>\n\n<p>My choice for screws was that bottom ones will be 10mm and top ones 20mm as that\xe2\x80\x99s enough space for B200 to be naturally cooled. After assembly it will look (hopefully) like this: </p>\n\n<p><a href="https://svbtleusercontent.com/yith78gpke3xvw.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://svbtleusercontent.com/yith78gpke3xvw_small.jpg" alt="b200_05.jpg"></a></p>\n\n<p>Since it\xe2\x80\x99s 45mm \xe2\x80\x9cfat\xe2\x80\x9d it can stand by side like this:<br>\n<a href="https://svbtleusercontent.com/bhqethcjrxxw.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://svbtleusercontent.com/bhqethcjrxxw_small.jpg" alt="b200_03.jpg"></a></p>\n\n<p>Bottom pic:<br>\n<a href="https://svbtleusercontent.com/t0hdyid7t1nl7w.jpg" rel="nofollow"><img src="https://svbtleusercontent.com/t0hdyid7t1nl7w_small.jpg" alt="b200_06.jpg"></a></p>\n\n<p>I do hope this helps you out and if so I would like to see your pictures of devices after they are done.
